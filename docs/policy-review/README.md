# Annual Policy Review Process

## Purpose

This document defines the annual review and re-approval process for organizational policies.

## Annual Review Schedule

All policies must be reviewed at least once every 12 months.

Policy owners are responsible for scheduling and completing reviews.

## Policy Review Process

Policies should be reviewed for:
- regulatory changes
- infrastructure changes
- operational updates
- security requirements

Policies should be updated as needed.

## Leadership Approval

Updated policies must be reviewed and approved by organizational leadership before publication.

## Communication to Employees

Policy changes should be communicated through:
- email notifications
- internal documentation systems
- employee awareness sessions

## Record Retention

Policy review records and approvals should be preserved for audit purposes.
